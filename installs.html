What to Know About Installs
Do I have to put any money down?

Usually, yes — for larger installs we'll ask for materials to be paid for up front, before we order anything or break ground.

Do you do landscape design?

If you need help visualizing a space, we can put together a small rendering concept so you can see what we're picturing before we start.

Do you do hardscapes?

Not really — the most we typically take on are small paver jobs. For larger hardscape projects, you'll want to go with a specialist.

I want a gravel refresh, but only a couple tons — is that possible?

That's fine, but keep in mind that if your yard has older gravel, we may not be able to find an exact match. Gravel comes from different veins at the quarry, so the color shifts slightly between batches — you may end up needing to cover the whole yard to make it look uniform.

Request a free quote — you don't even need to be home. We'll walk the property, talk through what you need, and follow up with your number.
