Tree Care

Healthy growth, well-timed.

Desert trees stay healthier — and safer — with regular, well-timed trimming. Our trim crew shapes and thins as part of your routine care, while a dedicated chipping crew clears every cut branch and hauls it away, so it doesn't sit piled in your yard.

What to Know About Tree Trimming
Why are trees over 15 ft billed separately?

Routine trimming up to 15 ft is covered under your maintenance contract. Taller trees take more time, bigger equipment, and more care to cut safely, so our crew trims them proactively whenever they judge it's needed — not something you have to request — and bills that work separately afterward.

What happens to all the debris?

We run a chipping crew separate from the crew doing the cutting. One team works through the tree while the other feeds the cut branches through the chipper and hauls debris off-site, so you're not left to deal with a pile of limbs yourself.

Do you handle storm damage or dead trees?

Yes — we have the crew and equipment to remove dead trees or trees that need to come down, not just trim them. Let us know what you're dealing with and we'll take care of it, billed separately from your maintenance contract.

Are you arborists?

No — we're landscapers, not certified arborists, so we won't diagnose tree disease or health issues. But for routine trimming, shaping, and removing a tree that needs to come down, we have the crew and equipment to handle it.

Anything else I should know?

Citrus and other tree fertilization is timed to the desert growing season and can be added right into your routine maintenance visits.
